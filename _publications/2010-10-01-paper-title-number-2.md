This Master's thesis investigates the associated production of two Higgs bosons with a top quark-antiquark pair (`ttÌ„HH`) using data from the CMS experiment at CERN. The analysis includes Monte Carlo simulations, background estimation, and deep learning methods to improve signal sensitivity.
